{# Please read the information carefully and be aware that you cannot go back to previous screens.#} Please read before accepting this HIT
  • This HIT is an academic study that takes approximately 10 minutes to complete.
  • You will earn $1,35 for completing the HIT. Also, you can earn up to an additional $2.70 depending on your decisions, another participant's decisions, and a random process.
  • The instructions at the start have four simple questions. If you or the other participant answers one of them incorrectly or takes exceptionally long to finish a page, both of you cannot participate any further.
  • In that case, the person who answered one of the four questions incorrectly or took exceptionally long to finish a page will receive no earnings, while the other person will receive $0.50.
  • If you and the other participant answer one of the four control questions correctly and complete the study, the last page will display a completion code. Please copy/paste that code in the box below so we can approve the HIT and transfer your earnings.
Data and consent
  • There is no physical discomfort nor any deception in this study.
  • Your decisions and the payment procedure are anonymous. None of the participants will learn the identity or the other participants’ earnings, and we will not ask for any personally identifying information.
  • Your data will only be used for academic purposes and will be treated as highly confidential. No individual responses will be shared. We will only analyze responses on the aggregate.
