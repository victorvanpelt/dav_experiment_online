  • This HIT is an academic study which takes approximately 10 minutes to complete.
  • You will earn $1,35 for completing the HIT. Also, you can earn up to an additional $2.70 depending on your decisions, another person's decisions, and a random process.
  • The instructions at the start of the HIT have four simple control questions. If you or the other person answers one of them incorrectly or takes exceptionally long to answer them, you cannot participate in this HIT and you will receive the base fee of $1.35.
  • If you and the other person answer the four control questions correctly and complete the study, the last page will display a completion code. Please copy/paste that code in the box below so we can approve the HIT and transfer your earnings.
Data and consent
  • There is no physical discomfort nor any deception in this study.
  • Your decisions and the payment procedure are anonymous. None of the participants will learn the identity or the other participants’ earnings, and we will not ask for any personally identifying information.
  • Your data will only be used for academic purposes and will be treated as highly confidential. No individual responses will be shared. We will only analyze responses on the aggregate.
